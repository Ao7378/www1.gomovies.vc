I Hear Your Voice

Trailer
Jang Hye Sung, who had a childhood in poverty and experienced painful memories, is a sassy, thick-faced, comical and materialistic public lawyer. She accidentally reunites with Park Soo Ha, who spent his first love for her after she decided to be a witness of his father's murder. Soo Ha has a supernatural ability of reading others' thoughts when he looks at their eyes, and he uses it to help Hye Sung with her cases. Hye Sung and Soo Ha also have to confront to Soo Ha's killer who has been released and now threatens them for revenge.
