Midnight Madness (CD1)

Trailer
Midnight Madness (CD1) (Also Known As: Competencia alocada ) is a comedy film directed by Michael Nankin, David Wechter and written by David Wechter, Michael Nankin. It released on 8 February 1980 (USA). The film stars David Naughton, Debra Clinger, David Damas. Leon planned "The Great Allnighter" by picking college students to participate in his night long scavenger hunt. The five teams, each designated by colors white (nerds who hate green),green (meat machine who hate red), red (sorority sisters who hate green), yellow (good guys who play fair), and blue (cheaters who hate yellow) are given clues to solve, leading them to the next clue site hidden in the city. Fox plays Scott,the younger brother,in his movie debut.
