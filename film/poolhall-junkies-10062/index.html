Poolhall Junkies ( Also Known As: Biliárd életre-halálra) is a Drama Thriller film directed by Mars Callahan and written by Mars Callahan, Chris Corso . It released on 28 February 2003 (USA) . The film stars Mars Callahan, Chazz Palminteri, Michael Rosenbaum.
