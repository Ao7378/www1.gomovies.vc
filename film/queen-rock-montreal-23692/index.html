Queen Rock Montreal

Queen Rock Montreal is a live album of Queen English. It was recorded at the Montreal Forum on November 24 and 25, 1981, exactly ten years before the death of soloist Freddie Mercury. This album marks the first official appearance of the We Will Rock You concert soundtrack in audio format.
